# Voice Note Ingest

The user's exact words are the signal.

## Rules

1. Preserve exact wording before summarizing.
2. Separate quote from interpretation.
3. Pull out tasks, product decisions, preferences, and reusable lessons.
4. Do not clean up the voice so much that intent changes.
5. If the note becomes a UMSMFBURASBOFE brief, keep must-not-break and proof lines explicit.
